About Ed-Ug

Transforming secondary education in Uganda

Our Mission

Ed-Ug provides quality education for all Ugandan secondary school students. We believe every student deserves access to excellent learning resources.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Ed-Ug was created by Ugandan educators to bridge the education gap through e-learning.

Our Values

  • Quality Education: High-quality content that meets standards.
  • Accessibility: Education available to everyone across Uganda.
  • Innovation: Using technology to enhance learning.
